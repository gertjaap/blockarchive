Where block files are located differs per chain and platform. The most common are listed here:

Bitcoin and its derivatives (Litecoin, Vertcoin, etc)

File name: blk*.dat

Windows: %APPDATA%\Roaming\{coin name}\blocks

Linux: ~/.{coin name}/blocks

MacOS: ~/Library/Application Support/{coin name}/blocks

Ethereum (Geth):

Windows: %APPDATA%\Roaming\Ethereum\chaindata

Linux: ~/.ethereum/chaindata

MacOS: ~/Library/Ethereum/chaindata
